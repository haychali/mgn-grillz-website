How it works

Simple 4-step process from idea to fitted grillz. No pressure, no awkward energy – just clean service and communication.

01 • Chat
DM & style decision
You send a DM with the look you want (pics welcome). We confirm teeth, style and budget.
02 • Fit
Mould appointment
We arrange a meet-up or pop-up slot. A mould is taken to match your teeth for a secure, comfortable fit.
03 • Create
Custom build
Your grillz are crafted based on your mould and design choices (tooth count, shape, finish).
04 • Fit Check
Collection & adjustments
You try on the grillz, we check comfort and look. Minor adjustments made if needed.

Questions men usually ask

Quick answers before you DM. If you’re unsure, just ask.

Do the grillz damage my real teeth?
No – they’re made to sit over your teeth. You still need good hygiene and to follow care instructions, but they’re not permanent.
Can I eat or sleep in them?
They’re designed for wear, photos and events – not for chewing food or sleeping. You’ll be told when to remove them.
How long does the process take?
After your mould, the build time depends on complexity. We’ll give you a realistic time frame when you book.
Is this only for men?
This page focuses on men’s styles, but we work with anyone. Just let us know the vibe you’re going for.
